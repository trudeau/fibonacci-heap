FIB-HEAP-EXTRACT-MIN(H)
 1  z ← min[H]
 2  if z ≠ NIL
 3      then for each child x of z
 4               do add x to the root list of H
 5                  p[x] ← NIL
 6           remove z from the root list of H
 7           if z = right[z]
 8              then min[H] ← NIL
 9              else min[H] ← right[z]
 10                   CONSOLIDATE(H)
 11           n[H] ← n[H] - 1
 12  return z
